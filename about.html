Our Story

Where passion meets craftsmanship

Born from Passion

Ember & Oak was founded in 2018 by coffee enthusiast Elena Martinez, who dreamed of creating a space where exceptional coffee and genuine human connection could flourish in the midst of our fast-paced urban landscape.

What started as a small corner shop has blossomed into a beloved community hub, where regulars know each other by name and newcomers instantly feel at home. Every detail, from our carefully sourced beans to the hand-picked vintage furniture, reflects our commitment to authenticity and quality.

Meet Elena

Elena Martinez, our founder and head roaster, fell in love with coffee during a transformative trip to Colombia in 2015. There, she witnessed firsthand the dedication of coffee farmers and the complex journey from seed to cup.

After returning home, Elena spent three years apprenticing with master roasters, perfecting her craft before opening Ember & Oak. Her vision was simple: create a space that honors the entire coffee journey while fostering genuine human connection.

"Coffee brings people together," Elena says. "Whether it's a morning ritual, an afternoon pick-me-up, or an evening conversation with friends, coffee is the thread that weaves through our daily lives. I wanted to create a place that celebrates that."
